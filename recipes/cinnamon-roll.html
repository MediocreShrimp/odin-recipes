Cinnamon Rolls

Description

These soft, fluffy cinnamon rolls are easy to make and perfect to serve during special occasions, or to serve as a breakfast treat!

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons quick rise or active yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk, at room temperature
  • ¼ cup butter, melted
  • 3 cups bread flour, plus more for dusting
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar (light brown sugar also works)
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter, softened

For the frosting:

  • 4 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 3 tablespoons butter, softened
  • ¾ cup powdered sugar
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
