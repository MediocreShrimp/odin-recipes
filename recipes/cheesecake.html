Oreo Cheesecake

Description

This simple, no-bake oreo cheesecake recipe is easy to make, rich and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 (19.1 oz) package OREO Cookies (divided)
  • 6 Tbsp butter (melted)
  • 4 (8oz) packages Philadelphia Cream Cheese (softened)
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 (8oz) tub Cool Whip Whipped Topping (thawed)

Steps

  1. Place about 15 of the cookies in a gallon size Ziploc bag. Crush the cookies using a rolling pin. You should still have some nice chunks.
  2. Place the remaining cookies into a food processor until they become finely crushed. Mix with butter.
  3. Place the finely crushed cookies onto bottom of a 13Ã—9-inch pan. Press them out evenly to form the crust. Refrigerate while preparing filling.
  4. Next, combine the cream cheese, sugar and vanilla in a stand mixer or with a hand mixer. Mix until well blended.
  5. Gently stir in whipped topping and chopped cookies. Spoon the batter over the crust and spread it out evenly. Cover.
  6. Refrigerate 4 hours or until firm.
